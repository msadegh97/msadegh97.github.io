A new preprint titled [Evaluating Sparse Interpretable Word Embeddings for Biomedical Domain](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.05114). We study the interpretability of the sparse word embedding methods on the medical domain and introduced a dataset for quantitative evaluation of interpretability for medical text.
